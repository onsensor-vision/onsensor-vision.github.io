Shinjeong Kim received the B.S. degree in Computer Science from KAIST, South Korea, in 2022, and the M.S. degree in Computer Science from ETH Zurich, Switzerland, in 2024. He is currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at Imperial College London, U.K. His research interests include 3D computer vision, spatial reasoning and their efficient implementations on novel sensing and robotic perception systems.
