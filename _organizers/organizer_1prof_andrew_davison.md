Professor Andrew Davison from Imperial College has worked for over 30 years in computer vision and robotics, and has experience of organising landmark workshops at conferences such as the Workshop on Live Dense Reconstruction at ICCV 2011, attended by over 250 people, and and Workshop on Distributed Algorithms for Robotics at ICRA 2023, attended by over 200. On Sensor Vision is now one of the key topics he is passionate about.
